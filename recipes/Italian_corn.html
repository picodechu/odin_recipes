Italian corn

Ingredients

    1 1/2 Tablespoons butter
    2 1/2cup whole kernel sweet corn
    1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
    1 Pinch salt and ground black pepper

How to prep

    Step 1.Heat a skillet over high heat. Heat butter in hot skillet until almost completely melted; stir in corn. Season corn with Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Cook seasoned corn until light golden brown, about 5 minutes.
