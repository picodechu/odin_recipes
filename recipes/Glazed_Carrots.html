Glazed Carrots

Ingredients

    Carrots Butter Brown sugar Seasonings

How to prep

    Step 1.Cook the carrots: Place the peeled and cut carrots in a saucepan ,cover with water, and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer until the carrots are tender. Drain. Step 2.Glazed the carrots: Melt the butter in a saucepan, then add the sugar, salt, and pepper. Add carrots and toss until well-coated.
